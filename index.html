About Me

    I graduated from the University of Missouri in May of 2022 with a bachelor of health science, with an emphasis in health and rehabilitation science. My career at Mizzou started with my pursuit of a degree in computer science, however, after my first year I decided to take a year off to reevaulate what it was I wanted to do with my life. After the year off I decided on pursuing physical therapy instead of software development. In my last two semester I did more research on the degree path and the programs I could get into and realized that the programs for this degree are more competitive and extensive than I orginally thought. With physical therapy out of the picture I tried picking up programming again by taking free courses on Codecademy.com. I refound the same passion I originally had for development and decided to continue my lessons while finishing up my health science degree.

    My focus has been on full-stack development with languages focused on that such as javascript, html, and css (see the skills tab for a more indepth breakdown). So far the projects that I have completed have been basic and prompt driven, however, I have started to move into more challenging tasks. The most ambitious one yet is this very website. I have designed this website to also be mobile compatible, as well as allowing it to show case the skills that I have developed in my short career.
